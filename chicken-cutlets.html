Crispy Chicken Cutlets

Chicken Cutlet
Oil Temp: 350°F Cook Time: 3–4 min / side Rest: 2 min

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Salt & pepper
  • Neutral oil

Instructions

  1. Pound chicken evenly.
  2. Season lightly.
  3. Bread: flour → egg → breadcrumbs.
  4. Fry at 350°F until golden.
  5. Drain and season immediately.

Best Uses

  • Carbonara
  • Chicken Parm
  • Lemon pasta
  • Sandwiches

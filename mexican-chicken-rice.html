Ingredients (Serves 4)

  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 lb chicken breast, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • ¬Ω cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook rice according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook chicken until browned and fully cooked.
  3. Add diced bell peppers and cook until slightly softened.
  4. Stir in taco seasoning and a splash of water; simmer 2‚Äì3 minutes.
  5. Divide rice into bowls and top with chicken mixture.
  6. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese and cilantro.
  7. Serve with lime wedges if desired.

Tips

  • Use fresh cilantro for a brighter flavor.
  • Add black beans or corn for extra texture and protein.
  • Adjust taco seasoning to taste ‚Äî less for mild, more for spicy.

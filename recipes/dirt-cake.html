Ben & Jerry’s Dirt Cake

Dessert Ice Cream No Bake
Watch me make this on Instagram
@knifetomeatu

Ingredients

  • 1½ quarts high-quality vanilla ice cream (not light)
  • 18–22 chocolate sandwich cookies (Oreos)
  • ½ cup chocolate syrup or chocolate fudge sauce
  • Optional but highly recommended:
  • ½ cup prepared vanilla pudding
  • Pinch of salt
  • Gummy worms (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Soften Ice Cream
    Remove vanilla ice cream from freezer and let sit 10–15 minutes until soft but not melted.
  2. Prepare Cookies
    Crush about ¾ of the cookies into fine crumbs. Break the remaining cookies into larger chunks. Set aside.
  3. Enhance Flavor (Optional Step)
    In a large bowl, gently stir the softened ice cream with vanilla pudding and a pinch of salt.
  4. Fold Ingredients
    Gently fold in cookie crumbs, cookie chunks, and chocolate syrup or fudge sauce. Swirl lightly — do not overmix.
  5. Layer
    Transfer half of the ice cream mixture into a freezer-safe container. Sprinkle with extra cookie crumbs and drizzle with chocolate syrup. Add remaining ice cream and finish with more crumbs on top.
  6. Freeze
    Cover and freeze for 4–6 hours until firm.
  7. Serve
    Let sit at room temperature for 5 minutes before scooping for best texture.

Optional “Topped” Version

  • After freezing 2–3 hours, spread a thin layer of melted chocolate mixed with 1 teaspoon coconut oil over the top.
  • Freeze again until hardened, then sprinkle with cookie crumbs before serving.

Ingredients

  • 4 small flour tortillas
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • Optional: fresh cilantro, sour cream, or hot sauce for topping

  5. Top with shredded Cheddar and Monterey Jack cheese.
  6. Bake in the oven for 8â€“10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from oven, garnish with cilantro, sour cream, or hot sauce as desired.
  8. Slice and serve warm.

Tips

  • Use pre-cooked chicken for quick assembly.
  • Try mixing cheeses for a creamier melt.
  • Optional: lightly toast the tortillas before adding toppings for extra crispiness.
